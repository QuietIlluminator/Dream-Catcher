Welcome to DreamCatcher!

This app is your one stop location for all of your dream based data and sleep pattern analysis. Use our app to track trends in your dreams and sleep, find sources of information, and better your night's sleep.


Sleep rests both your brain and your body, allowing for body recovery and emotion processing. Historically, dreams have been a significant part of culture, particularly dream sharing. Scientifically speaking, dreams allow for us to process our day's emotions, and therefore reflect on the events of the day. Stage 3 of the REM cycle is the period during which you will dream, normally in 90 minute cycles. Hence, tracking your sleep and dream patterns and understanding them is key to maintaining strong mental health. your mind to consolidate new memories from the day before with old material.
